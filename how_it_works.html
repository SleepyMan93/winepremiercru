How It Works

We are dedicated to transparent and dependable policies in every aspect of our business. Below you will find more detailed information on each of our core services. Should anything require further clarification, or if you have any questions whatsoever, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sell

1. Evaluation

We offer a full portfolio evaluation completely free of charge to each and every one of our clients. Once we have received details of your holdings we use the latest market analytics to produce a straightforward breakdown that clearly details the value of your portfolio within the current market.

2. Transfer

Once you have confirmed receipt of funds, we will issue full transfer instructions for your case or cases. We do of course request that you arrange for a swift release once funds have been safely received. Where necessary we will engage one of our trusted couriers to collect the case and deliver it on your behalf.

3. Sale

If you wish to proceed with a sale of some or all of your portfolio, we will market your wines to our global network of private buyers and trade partners utilising various sales channels, including direct B2B and B2C trades, spot offers to international markets, and various online sales platforms such as the world's largest fine wine exchange - Liv-Ex.

4. Payment

Once a sale is confirmed you will be issued a purchase order for your records at the previously agreed value for your case or cases. Payment terms will be confirmed with you in advance, with funds deposited into any account of your choosing in full within a set number of days. Please note the exact timeframe may vary, but will never exceed 30 days from the date our purchase order is issued.

Buy

1. Selection

We have a wide range of wines to choose from and we appreciate this can be a little daunting for new customers. If you require any advice on your fine wine purchases we are always on hand and happy to assist you. If you are looking for something specific please let us know and we will locate it for you.

2. Order

Once you have selected the wines you wish to purchase, please fill in the form on our website or give us a call to place your order. When the wines are confirmed we will issue an invoice for your records. Payment terms are generally set at 7 days from receipt, though this can be extended up to 30 days where necessary.

3. Payment

You can pay for your order via direct bank transfer or with any major credit card.

4. Delivery

We can arrange for worldwide delivery through one of our trusted couriers, please note that this will incur additional charges. If you would like to arrange for collection using your own courier you need only let us know.

5. Storage

You can request to have your wines shipped directly to any bonded storage facility of your choosing. Alternatively, we offer a range of managed portfolio storage services through our trade accounts at the pre-eminent bonded fine wine storage facility London City Bond - Vinotheque. For more information please do not hesitate to contact us.

Invest

Opportunity

Fine wine is an attractive way to diversify your investment portfolio whilst avoiding the tax implications and volatility of more traditional markets. We can offer tailored fine wine investment opportunities to all of our clients, regardless of their level of experience in the market or the status of their current portfolio. For further information regarding fine wine investment, we strongly recommend a meeting with one of our advisors to discuss available opportunities in more detail, including En Primeur offerings and newly released stock.

Our Story

Dominic Hall founded Wines Premier Cru in 2008 after many successful years as Chief Representative for GVA Worldwide in Beijing and Hong Kong. From humble beginnings managing only a few client portfolios and acting as an exclusive merchant of blue-chip stock from Bordeaux and Burgundy, Dominic's wealth of experience within the Asian market has been invaluable in developing Wines Premier Cru into the international business it is today.
Today, Wines Premier Cru manages a substantial range of private client portfolios and has developed relationships with leading chateau and suppliers worldwide. Dominic continues to drive the success of the business as Managing Director, with the help of a small, dedicated team of portfolio managers and sales executives.

Transparent

Dependable

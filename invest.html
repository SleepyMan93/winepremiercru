Invest in Fine Wine

Fine wine is an attractive way to diversify your investment portfolio whilst avoiding the tax implications and volatility of more traditional markets. The industry as a whole has outperformed the vast majority of other equities throughout the last 15 years, including during periods of economic downturn. This is in no small part due to two key factors which distinguish fine wine from other investment opportunities.

Investment Process

1
Build

Building your portfolio - Which wines you purchase depends on two key factors, your available capital and your desired timeframe for a return on your investment. Rest assured that there are options available for all investment levels. A discussion with one of our advisors will provide further information.

2
Storage

Storing your portfolio - You may wish to open your own bonded warehouse account in which to store your wines, however this will come with additional charges and some administrative responsibilities. WPC can offer a reduced rate for storing stock in our trade account at London City Bond as well as comprehensive portfolio management services for a small annual fee.

3
Returns

Rate of return - WPC offers a biannual stock report to every client displaying the position of your portfolio against the current market. Further detailed analysis is available on request, and we are always on hand to discuss your portfolio via email or telephone.

4
Sell

The decision to sell - The decision to sell rests with you and you alone. Contact our team day or night and we can arrange for a full or partial sale of your portfolio within a matter of weeks. For further information on our deliberately uncomplicated step-by-step sales process please visit our sales page here.

Buy

1. Selection

We have a wide range of wines to choose from and we appreciate this can be a little daunting for new customers. If you require any advice on your fine wine purchases we are always on hand and happy to assist you. If you are looking for something specific please let us know and we will locate it for you.

2. Order

Once you have selected the wines you wish to purchase, please fill in the form on our website or give us a call to place your order. When the wines are confirmed we will issue an invoice for your records. Payment terms are generally set at 7 days from receipt, though this can be extended up to 30 days where necessary.

3. Payment

You can pay for your order via direct bank transfer or with any major credit card.

4. Delivery

We can arrange for worldwide delivery through one of our trusted couriers, please note that this will incur additional charges. If you would like to arrange for collection using your own courier you need only let us know.

5. Storage

You can request to have your wines shipped directly to any bonded storage facility of your choosing. Alternatively, we offer a range of managed portfolio storage services through our trade accounts at the pre-eminent bonded fine wine storage facility London City Bond - Vinotheque. For more information please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sell Fine Wine

The team at Wines Premier Cru are portfolio sales specialists. From our preliminary evaluation through to funds being deposited into your account, we take pride in offering an efficient and reliable service from start to finish. The step by step sales process detailed below is deliberately uncomplicated and effective, yielding demonstrable returns within weeks of our preliminary evaluation.

Sell Your Portfolio
In 4 Easy Steps

Evaluation

Submit your portfolio below for a free evaluation of your current holdings utilising the latest market analytics.

Transfer

Once funds have been deposited into your account we will arrange for collection and delivery to take place through one of our trusted couriers.

Sale

Through our worldwide network of private buyers and trade partners we will arrange for your portfolio to be marketed and sold within a matter of weeks.

Payment

Funds will be deposited into your account in full before wines leave your ownership so that you are always in the position of control.
